Our history

Established in 2019, Sirdurx CodeLABS has been a trusted provider of educational software solutions. Our journey is marked by innovation, expertise, and a commitment to empowering students in their academic pursuits.

2019, SciExpo

In 2019, Sirdurx CodeLABS launched a virtual science fair platform, enabling students to showcase their research projects online. It facilitated interactive presentations and discussions, addressing the challenges of remote learning.

2020, MathCoLab

Sirdurx CodeLABS' app fostered student collaboration in math problem-solving. Real-time features and interactive tools facilitated strategy discussions, solution sharing, and skill development in a collaborative virtual environment.

2021, LinguaAI

Sirdurx CodeLABS' AI assistant revolutionized language learning. Personalized lessons, interactive exercises, and machine learning analysis optimized student progress and transformed the language learning experience.
